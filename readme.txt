PiPanel
=======
A XBMC skin optimized for the Raspberry Pi.

All the skins out there have a massive performance hit on the Raspberry Pi, PiPanel strive to change that.

What's PiPanel?
  1. Simple.
  2. Snappy.
  3. Stylish.

What isn't PiPanel?
  1. Graphics.
  2. The worlds most configureable skin.
  3. Finished.
